Dhudani Waterfall:

Dodhani waterfall is located near Dodhani village in Panvel taluka of Raigad District, Maharashtra, India. Dodhani waterfall lies at the base of the Matheran Mountain range. Dodhani waterfall falls in 3 Stages. This is a seasonal waterfall that dries completely after the Monsoon season.
